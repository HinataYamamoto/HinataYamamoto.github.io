My first project as a game developer, "Balling Ball", was inspired by one of my favorite video games, "Hotline Miami".
It won second place in one of the competitions held by my alma mater, "Vantan Game Academy High School".

"Balling Ball" is a top-down action game set in the alma mater, where you to move with W, S, A, and D, and destroy all enemies with "Adrenaline" which can be activated by holding right-click.
