"UP UP UP!" is a casual 2D action game with simple controls where you become a white ball and escape from a deep hole by jumping.

This game has great potential and won first place in one of the competitions held by "Vantan Game Academy High School", from which I graduated.
I plan to release this game on Google Play with many improvements in the future.
